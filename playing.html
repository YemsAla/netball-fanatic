Tips & Tricks

Here are the top 5 tips on essential netball skills - view each tile for useful tit-bits to help you learn how to play the game effectively.

Tip 1

Focus on Fundamentals

Master the basics of passing, shooting, and defending before moving on to advanced techniques. Practice regularly to build muscle memory and consistency in your skills.

Tip 2

Work on Footwork

Good footwork is essential in netball for maintaining balance, agility, and positioning. Practice footwork drills to improve your movement and ability to change direction quickly.

Tip 3

Communication is Key

Effective communication with your teammates is crucial in netball. Keep talking on the court to coordinate plays, call for passes, and provide support to your teammates.

Tip 4

Work on Footwork

Anticipate your opponent's movements and intentions to intercept passes and defend effectively. Stay focused and observant throughout the game to react quickly to changes in play.

Tip 5

Learn from Watching

Watch professional netball matches and analyze the techniques and strategies used by top players. Pay attention to their positioning, decision-making, and teamwork to improve your own game.
