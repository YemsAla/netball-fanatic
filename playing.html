Tutorials: Skills & Drills

Watch these tutorials to help you learn how to get the most of playing netball.

Passing

Tutorial: "Netball Passing Techniques" by Netball Australia

Description: This tutorial covers various passing techniques, including chest pass, shoulder pass, bounce pass and overhead pass. It provides step-by-step instructions and drills to improve passing accuracy and speed.

Shooting

Tutorial: "Netball Shooting Skills" by England Netball

Description: This tutorial focuses on shooting skills for goal shooters and goal attacks, covering shooting technique, footwork and accuracy. It includes drills and exercises to improve shooting consistency and confidence.

Defending

Tutorial: "Netball Defensive Techniques" by Netball New Zealand

Description: This tutorial explores defensive techniques for wing defence, goal defence, and goalkeeper positions. It covers positioning, marking, intercepting and defending against different types of attacks.

Footwork

Tutorial: "Netball Footwork Drills" by Netball Australia

Description: This tutorial focuses on footwork drills to improve agility, balance and coordination on the court. It includes exercises to practice pivoting, dodging and changing direction quickly.
